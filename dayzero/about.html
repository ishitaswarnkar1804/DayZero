What is DayZero?

DayZero is a calm digital space made for students who feel overwhelmed. It is not a productivity app. It is not a competition. It is a soft system to help you reset, refocus, and survive academic life one day at a time.

There are no leaderboards. No pressure. No noise. Just rooms that help you think, breathe, study, write, and let things out.

How it works

1. Login once

Your mood, goals, streak, and tasks are saved to your account.

2. Pick a vibe

The theme you choose follows you across every room.

3. Add tiny tasks

To-dos reset daily. Finished ones disappear. Unfinished ones stay.

4. Show up

Your streak grows when you return. Miss a day? It resets gently.

Your data

Your login data, mood, goals, streak, and tasks are saved to your account. Notes, rants, and library annotations are stored locally in your browser.

DayZero does not track you. It does not judge you. It only remembers what you ask it to.

You are allowed to go slow.

DayZero is not here to make you faster. It is here to make you kinder to yourself.
